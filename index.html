DHL - America's Cup

DHL America's cup is an international sporting event where 37 countries come together for a football and cheerleading event like no other. With over 1,600 attendees from across the America's it was important for the event to run smoothly and efficiently.

I was tasked whilst at Brand Brewery to design and help build an interactive app which would help attendees keep to schedule, view scores and results and be notified with key information whilst at the event in real time.

Doki Doki

Doki Doki is an app I created whilst studying UX design with CalArts. I was tasked with creating an interactive prototype within the travel sector. I opted to design an itinerary planner specifically for use in Japan.

My app allowed users to create specific itineraries based on their interests with the idea to help tourists live like a local and discover new neighbourhoods
